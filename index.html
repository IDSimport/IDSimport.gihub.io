Where It All Began

IDS Import Inc was founded in 2022 with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between quality international products and Latin American markets. From the very beginning, we focused on sourcing and distributing goods across multiple categories — groceries, tools, skincare, and more — delivering real value to businesses throughout the region.

🌎

Expansion

Growing Our Reach

After establishing a strong distribution network in Latin America, we identified an opportunity to scale. We began expanding our operations, strengthening supplier relationships and developing new logistical capabilities to serve a broader client base efficiently and reliably.

🇺🇸

Today & Beyond

Entering the American Market

Today, IDS Import Inc is actively expanding its reach across the entire United States market. Based in Florida — a strategic hub for international trade — we are positioned to serve American businesses with the same commitment to quality, reliability, and exceptional service that has defined us since day one.
